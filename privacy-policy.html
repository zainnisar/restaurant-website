Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Guildford Mina Pizzeria ("we", "us" or "our") runs this website to share our menu, deals, location and opening hours with the Guildford community. We know a privacy policy isn't the most exciting page on a pizza website, but we want to be upfront about what happens when you visit, so here it is in plain language.

What this website collects

This website doesn't ask you to fill out any forms, sign up for anything, or create an account, so we don't directly collect names, email addresses or phone numbers through the site itself.

Like almost every website, some basic technical information is recorded automatically when you visit, such as your general location, browser type, device type and the pages you looked at. This is handled by our hosting provider (Netlify) as part of running the site securely and reliably, and it isn't used by us to identify you personally.

Third party services on this site

A few outside services help this website work properly, and each of them has its own privacy practices that sit outside our control:

  • Google Fonts is used to load the typefaces on this page, which means your browser makes a request to Google's servers.
  • Google Maps is embedded in the Visit section so you can find us easily. Google may set its own cookies or collect data when the map loads.
  • NextOrder is the platform we use for online ordering. When you click "Order Online", you leave our website and go to NextOrder's own site, where you may be asked for details like your name, phone number and delivery address to complete an order. That information is handled under NextOrder's privacy policy, not ours.
  • Facebook and Instagram links in our footer take you to our social pages, which are governed by Meta's privacy policies.

Cookies

We don't set our own tracking or advertising cookies on this website. The third party services mentioned above, particularly Google Maps, may set cookies of their own when their content loads on the page.

How we use information

Any technical information collected through hosting is used only to keep the website running, secure and fast. We don't sell, rent or share information about our visitors, and we don't run advertising or tracking campaigns through this website.

Children's privacy

This website is a general audience site about our restaurant and isn't directed at children. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children through this site.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to ask what personal information a business holds about you and to have it corrected if it's wrong. Since this website doesn't collect personal information directly, this mostly applies to information you might give us in person, over the phone, or through the NextOrder platform when ordering.

If you have any concerns about how your information has been handled, you're welcome to contact us using the details below. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can also raise the matter with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time if the way our website works changes. Any updates will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
