About Us

A Guildford institution, day and night.

At Guildford Mina Pizzeria, we believe great pizza starts with great ingredients. Our passion is bringing people together over freshly made pizzas, crafted with quality toppings, rich sauces and perfectly baked dough. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or sharing a meal with family and friends, every pizza is made with care and a love for authentic flavour. We're proud to be part of the Guildford community, One slice at a time, we're dedicated to delivering the taste, quality, and experience that keeps our customers coming back.
